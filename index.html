FAQ

Questions employers ask before switching pension provider.

Does Zen replace Auto-Enrolment?

Yes. A PRSA through Zen qualifies as a workplace pension, satisfying Ireland's pension obligations for eligible staff. For most businesses it is also a more flexible route than the default state scheme, with greater portability and no state top-up restrictions.

What does it cost employers?

There are no employer setup fees, no administration charges and no monthly platform fees. Your business still contributes the pension amounts it chooses to make for staff. Employees pay an annual management fee of 0.70% to 0.90% depending on their balance size.

How long does setup take?

Most employers are live in under 15 minutes. Setup starts with a payroll upload. From there, Zen handles onboarding, contribution tracking and employee access automatically, with no manual follow-up needed from your side.

What is a PRSA?

A PRSA is a Personal Retirement Savings Account, a pension structure regulated and approved by the Pensions Authority. Zen uses a PRSA to provide a workplace pension, which means employees own and carry their pension with them if they change jobs.

What happens when an employee leaves?

Their pension remains theirs. Because Zen uses a PRSA structure, the pension is fully portable. Employees keep visibility and control over their savings no matter where their career takes them.

Is employee pension money secure?

Yes. Zen is regulated by the Central Bank of Ireland and approved by the Pensions Authority. Employee assets are held by State Street, a global custodian responsible for safeguarding trillions in financial assets worldwide, and the pension is backed by Acorn Life DAC, giving employees serious institutional safeguards behind the modern app experience.

Can employees track everything in the app?

Yes. The app shows pension balance, contributions, investment performance and all documents in one place. Employees can also access in-app support without calling HR or filling in any forms.

What support is available after setup?

Most pension questions are answered directly in the app, so they stop landing on HR's desk. When a human answer is needed, the Zen team is there for both employers and employees.
